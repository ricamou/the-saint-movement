Creator Fund

Fueling builders, storytellers, and community growth.

The Creator Fund is a future initiative designed to support people who help grow the $SAINT movement with quality content, outreach, and community contribution.

Purpose

Growth should reward contribution.

The fund is not active yet. This page defines the intended framework so the community understands the direction before implementation.

01

Creators

Support for videos, threads, graphics, explainers, and educational content that strengthens the movement.

02

Builders

Recognition for people who contribute ideas, tools, community resources, and helpful infrastructure.

03

Community

Potential community nominations and voting when governance systems are ready.

How it may work

A transparent process.

Future submissions could be reviewed openly, selected by clear criteria, and tracked publicly so the community can see how support is distributed.

Contribution should be visible. Support should be accountable.
